Starter pokemon are the first pokemon that the player recieve in the region. These cute pokemon can evolve into strong competetors in a fight.

Some pokemon are able to have multiple evolutions depending on certain conditions. Eevee is a pokemon capable of having 8 deffernet evolutions, each effective in their own way.

Fossils of prehistoric pokemon can be found on the player's journey. These are able to be revived and used as strong pokemon with prehistoric powers.

The Ultra Beasts are a group of pokemon not from this dimension. These strong pokemon posses power that transcends humans' understanding of the world.

Psuedo-Legendary Pokemon refer to strong pokemon with a base stat total of exactly 600. These pokemon are often stronger than most other non legendary pokemon and can devistate an opposing team.

Legendary Pokemon are some of the strongest pokemon in the franchise. There are normally only one of each per game so make sure you catch it while you can!
